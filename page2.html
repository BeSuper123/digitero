1. Fortnite

Fortnite is a shooter, survival game. The main point of the game is that there are 100 players put on an island and you have to be the last one standing, while outlasting the storm. You can use building to help you in this game

It is a great game to play with frends and I really recommend it for people who like a good challenge and are competitive. It is also great for peope who like doing creative type of games too.



2. Call Of Duty: Modern Warfare 2

Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2 is a first person shooter game. It has different modes such as campaign, Spec Ops, Multiplayer, Core, and Warzone.

It is a great game and I really recommend this game for competitive people who want a challenge.



3. Apex Legends

Apex Legends is an online multiplayer battle royale game where solos, duos, or squads use characters with different abilities called "Legends", battle it out until they are the last ones standing.

It is a great game to play with frends and I really recommend it for people who like a good challenge and are competitive.



4. Valorant

Valorant is a team based, first person hero shotter game. The player plays as one of the "Agents" in the game. The Agents have special abilities. In the main mode, players are put on either the attacking or defending team. Only one team wins.

It is a fantasic game and I really recommend this game for competitive people who are good at working with others.



5. Warframe

Warframe is a third person game where you go alone or with team members to fight off enemies and advanced robots. The player takes on the role of a tenno, an ancient warrior created by Orokin to fight off a mysterious foe but left to sleep generations ago. The player picks one of three tenno and fight on different planets.

It is an amazing game and I really recommend it for people who like fighting off based on a storyline. It is also great for people who like working with others.



6. Overwatch

Overwatch is a team based action game set in the future. Every match is an ultimate 5v5 battlefield brawl. You fight as heroes with different maps to ecplore.

It is a great game and I really recommend this game for people who like using different characters and like playing with others.
